The constant neutrals

These don't change per direction — warm paper (Wispr), charcoal wells (your terminal), muted semantics (Linear). Hairlines instead of shadows.

frame#ECEBE8
sheet#FBFAF8
sidebar#F4F3F0
well (terminal)#16181C
ink#1B1A17
ink-2#6E6C66
ink-3#A3A199
ok#3D9A63
warn#C7912B
danger#C4483C

Usage rules (whichever direction wins)

1 · Accent appears at most three times per screen: active nav, one primary action, one live status.
2 · Buttons are INK (dark pill) by default — accent is reserved for the single most important action, Wispr-style.
3 · The capture pulse is always accent: it is the brand moment (like Wispr's fn keycap).
4 · Semantic colors only ever appear as small chips/dots, never as backgrounds.
5 · Text on paper is never pure black; text on wells is never pure white.
